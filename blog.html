Week 1.

At this moment in time I have zero knowledge of any programming languages, but I am willing to apply myself to my development. My aim is to keep up with the others and the tutors along the way. Through the lessons so far. I have learned how the web works and potential career opportunities, skills and salaries available to me should I complete this programming course.
